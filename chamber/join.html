Membership Level Benefits for Saint George Chamber of Commerce

Non-Profit (NP) Membership


  • No fee for non-profit organizations.
  • Access to networking events and business development opportunities.
  • Inclusion in the Chamber's directory and online listing, showcasing your organization to a wide audience.
  • Opportunities for collaboration and partnerships with other non-profit and business members.

Bronze Membership


  • Affordable membership fee.
  • All the benefits of NP Membership, plus:
  • Exclusive invitations to Chamber-sponsored events, including business luncheons, workshops, and seminars.
  • Increased visibility through featured listings in the Chamber's directory and website.
  • Promotion of your organization's news, events, and achievements through the Chamber's communication channels.

Silver Membership


  • Membership fee at a higher investment.
  • All the benefits of Bronze Membership, plus:
  • Priority access to the Chamber networking events and industry-specific workshops.
  • Enhanced exposure with featured spotlights on the Chamber's home page and social media platforms.
  • Display of your organization's marketing materials at Chamber events and the Chamber office.

Gold Membership


  • Membership fee offering the highest level of benefits and premium exposure.
  • All the benefits of Silver Membership, plus:
  • VIP access to exclusive Chamber events, including high-profile receptions and galas.
  • Premier positioning with top-tier logo placement on the Chamber's website and promotional materials.
  • Direct engagement with influential business and community leaders through executive networking events.
